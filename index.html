Why plan?

"If you fail to plan, you are planning to fail." - Benjamin Franklin. Simple financial forecasting can go a long way, even if things change rapidly or are uncertain.


Manage cash

Make sure you don’t run out of money tomorrow and when/if you’ll need to raise more

Allocate spending

Let your team know what each can spend while insuring you can cover all costs

Get financing

Investors, lenders, potential acquirers need forecasts to evaluate their investment and anchor valuation

Plan ahead

Hiring, office space, inventory require lead time. Know when you'll need (or can afford) them

Set goals

Align everyone on concrete goals and think through the details to reach them. Create accountability.

ROI

Put some numbers behind where you're planning to invest resources to estimate return on investment
